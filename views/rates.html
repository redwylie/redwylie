Session Rates

Headshot Sessions:

Headshot session rate is $300 for the shoot.

There is also a discounted fee for kids 16 and under or returning clients of $200.

Portrait and Headshot Sessions:

Portrait and Headshot session rate is $500 for the shoot.

There is also a discounted fee for kids 16 and under or returning clients of $300.

Included with each session:

Headshot sessions include retouching of 2 files.
Portrait with Headshot sessions include retouching of 4 files.

Additonal photo retouching can be requested at an additional cost of $60 per photo.

You may also elect to purchase a full buyout of the entire session for $200. If you choose this option, you will receive retouching of 2 to 4 files and all the remaining photos unretouched in camera RAW format, along with jpgs.

Scheduling

I am avaliable Monday through Friday evenings beginning at 7:00 pm, as well as Saturday and Sunday between 10:00 am and 7:00 pm.

Be sure to review the details to help you get an idea about the photo sessions.
